About

Zuss esport is a bangi-based esports organization that has gained attention in various competitive gaming scenes. The history of Zuss esports in virtual sports has been growing since 2022. This organization was founded by 3 people namely Khairul amin, Harith nasuha and Azham hakimi. Initially they focused on the game Valorant which is a tactical first person shooter (FPS) game. However, with the development of this organization, the game in this esport has been expanded with various other games such as Pubg, mobile legend, dota 2 and fortnite..

History

The history of Zuss Esport began when 3 friends dreamed of creating an esports company after finishing SPM to achieve success in the field of gambling. It was established in Bangi at Harith Nasuha's house at night. This idea comes about because esports is the most effective field for the current age of technology, it can attract young people to earn money through games.
